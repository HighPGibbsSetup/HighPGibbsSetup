HighPGibbs is a practical tool that combines the deep earth water model (DEW) with the Gibbs minimization calculator of the HCh. It solves the problem of geochemical calculation of fluid-rock systems as an integral model and can simulate the equilibrium between aqueous solution and minerals (with solid solutions) up to 1200 Â°C and 60 kbar.

Zhong R. C., Li Y. X., Etschmann B., Brugger J., Yu C., Cui H., 2019. HighPGibbs, a practical tool for fluid-rock thermodynamic simulation in deep earth and its application on calculating nitrogen speciation in subduction zone fluids. Geochemistry, Geophysics, Geosystems, Submitted manuscript.
